Travel to the Maldives

Maldives is the perfect place to reboot and take a break from the hustle and bustle of the city. Entertainment and active leisure are mainly related to nature and the ocean, and the tourist season here lasts almost all year round.

Top 3 places to visit!

  1. Male

    The tiny Maldivian capital, which is the driving force behind the life of the entire archipelago, has a lot to offer its guests. Surrounded by a turquoise sea, this city is great for walking without a pre-planned route.

  2. Nalaguraidu Island

    Nalaguraidu, with its charming blue waters, sparkling beaches and fabulous resorts, guarantees its guests immeasurable pleasure.

    The main activities on the island

    • diving
    • windsurfing and snorkeling
    • relaxing in the sauna and SPA

  3. Banana Reef

    The banana-shaped reef impresses with its fantastic corals, amazing rocks, ledges and caves. For adventurers, this is one of the most attractive places in the Maldives.

    The main activities on the island

    • diving
    • windsurfing and snorkeling
